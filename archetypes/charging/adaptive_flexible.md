# Adaptive / Flexible Charging

Information Engagement: 3/5
Risk Buffer Preference: 3/5
Adaptive Flexibility: 5/5
Cost/Effort Willingness: 4/5

This person changes plans quickly when charging conditions shift. They are comfortable changing station, route, timing, or activity order when queues, outages, traffic delays, or incomplete information make the original plan less attractive.

Under uncertainty, they look for workable alternatives rather than staying fixed on the first plan. They may swap the order of activities, use a different charging stop, take a moderate detour, or adjust timing to keep the day moving.

They do not require the highest possible buffer, but they respond actively to changing conditions. Their choices should show practical replanning and willingness to trade some effort for a smoother outcome.
