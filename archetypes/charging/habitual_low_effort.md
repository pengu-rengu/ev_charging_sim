# Habitual / Low-effort Charging

This person uses little charging information and avoids active comparison between stations. They prefer familiar, nearby, or convenient stations and tend to stay with a routine even when better options may exist.

Under uncertainty, they make simple choices: continue to the known station, accept the first convenient option, or avoid extra effort unless the current state forces action. They are unlikely to check several apps, reroute aggressively, or pay extra for a carefully optimized option.

If a station is unavailable, has a long queue, or has incomplete information, they may wait briefly, choose another very familiar option, or give up on charging until it becomes unavoidable.
