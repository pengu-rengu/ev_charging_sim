# Information-seeking Charging

This person actively checks charging information before deciding. They compare station availability, queue length, outage reports, connector type, travel time, and route fit before choosing where to go.

Under uncertainty, they prefer decisions backed by current information. They may delay a decision briefly to check conditions, choose a station with clearer status, or compare several options before committing.

They can adapt when information changes, but they do not always choose the most expensive or longest-detour option. Their behavior should show active search and comparison rather than automatic routine.
