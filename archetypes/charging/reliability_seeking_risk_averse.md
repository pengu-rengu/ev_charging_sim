# Reliability-seeking / Risk-averse Charging

This person prioritizes avoiding charging failure. They maintain a high state-of-charge buffer and are willing to accept extra cost, time, or distance to reduce the chance of being stranded or missing a necessary trip.

Under uncertainty, they choose the option with the highest reliability even when it is less convenient. They may pay for a faster or more dependable station, leave earlier, avoid low-SOC exposure, or detour to a station with stronger availability.

If a station has a long queue, outage risk, or incomplete information, they prefer a backup with a better chance of success. They are less tolerant of gambling on marginal range or uncertain infrastructure.
